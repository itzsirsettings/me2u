## Design Context

### Users
Me2U is for individuals and cooperatives seeking trust-based, interest-free peer lending. Users are likely security-conscious and value transparency and community. They use the platform to manage personal finances, borrow responsibly, or earn rewards through referrals.

### Brand Personality
**Refined, Secure, Trustworthy.** 
The voice is confident and institutional but accessible (Apple Card-esque). It avoids "startup hype" and instead projects stability and premium quality.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Minimalist Monochrome.**
The visual tone is clean, spacious, and typography-focused. It uses a warm monochrome palette with strategic brand blue accents. It explicitly avoids "AI slop" (no neon gradients, no generic card stripes, no overused Inter-like fonts).

### Design Principles
1. **Premium Precision**: Every pixel, margin, and type choice must feel intentional. Avoid generic defaults.
2. **Institutional Security**: The UI should feel as stable and secure as a high-end bank, using clear hierarchy and stable layouts.
3. **Respect the Core**: The brand logo (84px in nav) and current colors (`#00406b`, `#007fff`, etc.) are foundational. They must be showcased, not altered.
4. **Purposeful Motion**: Use motion to clarify and delight (staggered reveals, smooth decelaration), but never at the expense of clarity or financial seriousness.
5. **Mobile-First Luxury**: The experience must feel "handcrafted" on mobile, especially the phone mockup which is the centerpiece of the hero.
